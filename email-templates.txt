TIPS FOR USING THESE TEMPLATES:
==========================================

1. PERSONALIZE: Always customize [bracketed] sections with specific names and details
2. RESEARCH: Look up the business/contact before sending - mention something specific
3. TIMING: Send emails Tuesday-Thursday, 9 AM - 2 PM for best response rates
4. FOLLOW UP: If no response in 1 week, send Template 6 (follow-up)
5. BE CONCISE: Keep emails short and to the point
6. CALL TO ACTION: Always include a clear next step
7. PROOFREAD: Check for typos before sending
8. TRACK: Use the spreadsheet to track who you've contacted and when

==========================================
HOW TO FIND CONTACT INFORMATION:
==========================================

1. Google: "[Business Name] St. Thomas contact"
2. Check their website for contact forms or email
3. Use Google Maps - many businesses list contact info
4. LinkedIn: Search for business owners/managers
5. Facebook: Many businesses have contact info on their page
6. Yelp: Often has phone numbers listed
7. Call directly: Sometimes calling is faster than email
