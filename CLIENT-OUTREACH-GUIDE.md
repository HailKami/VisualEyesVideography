# Client Outreach Guide - VisualEyes Videography

## Files Created:
1. **client-tracking.csv** - Spreadsheet to track all potential clients
2. **email-templates.txt** - Ready-to-use email templates for outreach

## Quick Start Guide:

### Step 1: Open the Spreadsheet
- Open `client-tracking.csv` in Excel, Google Sheets, or Numbers
- Fill in contact information as you find it
- Update status as you contact people

### Step 2: Find Contact Information
Use these methods:
- **Google Maps**: Search business name + "St. Thomas" - often shows phone/email
- **Business Websites**: Check "Contact" or "About" pages
- **Google Search**: "[Business Name] St. Thomas contact email"
- **LinkedIn**: Search for business owners/managers
- **Facebook**: Many businesses list contact info
- **Yelp**: Often has phone numbers

### Step 3: Use Email Templates
- Open `email-templates.txt`
- Copy the template that matches your target
- Replace [bracketed] sections with specific details
- Personalize it - mention something specific about their business
- Send!

### Step 4: Track Your Progress
- Update the spreadsheet after each contact
- Set follow-up dates (usually 1 week after initial contact)
- Note any responses or conversations

## Tips for Success:

1. **Personalize Every Email**: Mention something specific about their business
2. **Be Persistent**: Follow up if no response (but don't be annoying)
3. **Offer Value**: Focus on how you can help them, not just what you want
4. **Local Connection**: Emphasize your local knowledge and experience
5. **Portfolio**: Always include link to your website
6. **Easy Contact**: Make it easy for them to reach you (phone, email)

## Tracking Status Options:

Use these statuses in your spreadsheet:
- **Not Contacted** - Haven't reached out yet
- **Contacted** - Sent initial email
- **Follow-up Sent** - Sent follow-up email
- **Responded** - They replied (positive or negative)
- **Meeting Scheduled** - Set up a call/meeting
- **Partnership Formed** - Working together!
- **Not Interested** - They declined (move on)

## Sample Workflow:

1. Find business on Google Maps
2. Get contact info from website/Google
3. Add to spreadsheet
4. Copy appropriate email template
5. Personalize and send
6. Update spreadsheet status
7. Set calendar reminder for follow-up (1 week)
8. Follow up if no response

## Remember:

- **Quality over Quantity**: Better to send 5 personalized emails than 20 generic ones
- **Be Patient**: Building relationships takes time
- **Stay Organized**: Use the spreadsheet to track everything
- **Follow Up**: Most people need a reminder
- **Be Professional**: Represent your brand well

Good luck! 🎬
